JUSTIFIED INTERNATIONAL CHURCH

Our purpose is to reflect God's glory.

By Debs

May 31, 2024

Justified International Church is a Bible believing Church. This means the Word of God is the final authority over our lives. Our opinions and our desires do not matter in the face of what God has commanded us to do. Our anchor Scripture is found in the Gospel of Matthew 5:13-16:

“"You are the salt of the earth; but if the salt loses its flavor, how shall it be seasoned? It is then good for nothing but to be thrown out and trampled underfoot by men. "You are the light of the world. A city that is set on a hill cannot be hidden. Nor do they light a lamp and put it under a basket, but on a lampstand, and it gives light to all who are in the house. Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works and glorify your Father in heaven.”

We are equiped with kingdom hearted leaders.
